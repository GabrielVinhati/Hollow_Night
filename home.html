Are You Ready to Embark on This Journey?

Venture into the forgotten kingdom of Hallownest, a world full of mystery, danger, and untold stories. As the enigmatic Knight, you'll face challenges, conquer fierce foes, and uncover the secrets buried deep beneath the surface. The question is: are you ready to embrace the unknown and uncover the truth hidden in the darkness?

Welcome to Hollow Knight

The Radiance
The Light Beyond the Void

The Radiance is a being of light and the true antagonist of Hollow Knight. Forgotten over time, it resurges as the source of the infection plaguing Hallownest. Its battle is a true test of endurance and skill, challenging players with fast attacks and unpredictable patterns. Defeating it is the only way to rid the kingdom of corruption and end the cycle of suffering.

Pure Vessel
The Hollowed Perfection

The Pure Vessel is the untainted version of the Hollow Knight, showing what it could have been before being corrupted by the infection. This fight is a true duel between warriors, with extremely fast and aggressive movements. The confrontation against the Pure Vessel symbolizes the fight against an inevitable fate, where only determination can lead to victory.

The Eternal Performance

Nightmare King Grimm is the enhanced version of the leader of the Grimm Troupe, a mysterious entity that collects flames to sustain its existence. His battle is one of the most intense in the game, requiring fast reflexes and precision. With fiery attacks and sudden appearances, facing Grimm feels like participating in a deadly performance, where only the most skilled can survive.

Hollow Knight:
Godmaster

Hollow Knight: Godmaster is the final and most challenging free expansion for the game, introducing new bosses, trials, and game modes. Players venture into the realm of the gods, facing brutal battles in a series of pantheons that test their skills against enhanced versions of existing bosses and powerful new foes. The DLC expands the game's lore, uncovering secrets about divine beings and the Knight’s true purpose. With epic fights and additional endings, Godmaster is the ultimate challenge for those seeking to master the world of Hallownest.
